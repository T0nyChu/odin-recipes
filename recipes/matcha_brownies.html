MATCHA BROWNIES

Matcha and brownies are a great combination for an irresistible treat.

Easy to bake with simple ingredients!

Perfect to eat anytime and can be made to be cakey or chewy.

Ingredients:

  • Matcha powder
  • Unsalted Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • White Chocolate Chips
  • Salt

How to make:

  1. Mix flour, matcha, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Mix butter, brown sugar, 2 eggs in a separate bowl.
  3. Pour wet mixture into dry mixture and stir in white chocolate chips.
  4. Grease baking pan with butter and pour brownie mixture in.
  5. Decorate with white chocolate chips and walnut.
  6. Bake for 25 min at 320F.
  7. Let brownie cool for 30min - 1hr.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
