EGG TARTS

One of the most scrumptious and satisfying dessert tart.

Buttery Crust and Eggy Goodness.

Best when freshly baked out of the oven.

Ingredients:

Tart Pastry

  • All-purpose flour (200g)
  • Powder sugar (75g)
  • Unsalted butter (140g)
  • Egg (1/2ea)

Egg Custard

  • Egg (2ea)
  • Sugar (50g)
  • Water (150mL)
  • Milk (50mL)

How to make:

  1. Boil water and add sugar and milk.
  2. Mix well and set aside to cool to room temperature.
  3. Mix flour and sugar in a separate bowl.
  4. Knead the soft butter into the bowl.
  5. Add the beaten egg and knead into a dough.
  6. Place dough onto cling film and flatten it.
  7. Place flatten dough into fridge for around 30min.
  8. Mix the cooled milk mixture with the beaten eggs and seive it.
  9. Take the cold dough out of the fridge and roll until 6mm thickness.
  10. Use a circle cookie cutter to cut out the dough.
  11. Place the cut-out dough on a tart mold and gently shape the dough.
  12. Place in the fridge for 15min or until dough is hardened.
  13. Pour in custard mixture to 80% full.
  14. Preheat oven to 180C(355F) and bake for 20-25 min.
  15. Use a toothpick to check if custard is cooked properly.
  16. Let it cool and enjoy!
